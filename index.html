How to upload files to Google Drive

Step-1: Go to Google Drive and click on "New File". Then click on file.
Step-2: Locate your file and tap on upload. Wait until the process is finished.
Finally your file is uploaded to Google Drive.

How to get Google Drive share link

Step-1: Go to Google Drive and locate the file that you want to share. Now right click on it and then click on "Get shareable link".
Step-2: Make sure you selected "Anyone with the link". If selected, click on the "Copy link" button.
That's it!
